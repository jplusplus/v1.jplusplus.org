Celebrating 2 years of Journalism++

Major events in year 2

On December 13th, 2013, Journalism++ turns 2! In the last 12 months, we shared our brand with 3 other companies in Stockholm, Amsterdam and Cologne. Together, we form the best datajournalism network in Europe and beyond.

We released several open-source tools for journalists, like our Benford's Law checker, Spending Stories, Broken Promises and Detective.io. ABZV's Datawrapper, to which we contribute, saw its usage double.

We remained committed to hard-hitting journalism, contributing to investigations on innovators in developing countries, autistic kids, the TPP or US diplomacy.

We spread the word about datajournalism and helped many get to grips with Excel, OpenRefine and Javascript. From France's journalism schools to Belgrade to Lilongwe to Kathmandu, we moved more in 2013 than at any other time in our lives.

Our most sincere thanks to all our clients, partners and friends. You're the ones who made it possible.

Stay tuned and watch our Github repositories for upcoming goodness, for 2014 will be even better!
